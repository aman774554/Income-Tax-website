Gender as per PAN details

  • Please verify if the Name, Date of Birth, Gender as per the Aadhar card matches the detail as per your PAN data. Enteryour Aadhar number in the text box provided below and Click on "Link Aadhar" button
  • Income Tax Department will validate your Name, Date of birth, Gender as per the Aadhar Number with UIDAI. Please note that if the details do not match, the Aadhar Number will not linked to your PAN.
  • Once the validation is successful, your Aadhar will be successfully linked to your PAN.
